About

Dana Kim (김단아, b. 1993) is a new media artist and researcher, currently a master's student at the Graduate School of Metaverse, KAIST. She holds a BA in Communication from Yonsei University and previously worked in marketing planning at KT (Korea Telecom).

Since her undergraduate studies, her interests have centered on cultural phenomena and methods of communication, which she has explored through a series of short films and documentaries — including The Time of Walking in the Forest Path (2023). Her recent work overlays her own experiences of the city's public spaces.

She seeks ways to communicate effectively with audiences and to create audience-driven experiences, and plans to expand her methods by focusing on cultural-anthropological phenomena.
